Game Overview

Clash Royale is a popular real-time strategy game developed and published by Supercell, the creators of Clash of Clans. It combines elements from collectible card games, tower defense games, and multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games into a unique gaming experience.

Clash Royale has evolved a lot over the years with addition of new cards as well as arenas. There has also cbeen introduction of new game formats alongwith the introduction of cards with new rarity.

Game Highlights

Some memorable moments in Clash Royale include the annual Clash Royale League, where top players and teams from around the world compete for the championship.

Some other important events in the history of Clash Royale are:

  • Clash Royale was launched in March 2016. The idea of Card-based strategy game and real-time multiplayer battles helped it in gathering popularity initally.

  • Crown Championship was the first official Clash Royale esports competition, culminating in the World Finals in London. The event displayed the competitive potential of the game and played a significant role in establishing Clash Royale in the field of E-Sports.

  • The Clash Royale League(CRL) was launched in 2018 wherein, famous players from all over the world compete. In addition to this, the game was also updated this year and Clan Wars was added which allowed the players to form groups and play.

  • One of the most memorable moments in the history of Clash Royale is the victory of Surgical Goblin in 2017 World Finals.

How to Get Started

To get started with Clash Royale, download the game from your app store, create an account, and start learning the basics of gameplay, such as managing your elixir and deploying troops strategically.

You also need to gradually know about the different cards/characters in the game and know about their features and how they work when played together. Also, it is important to know about the combination of cards which when played together increases the power.

It is advisable for the beginners to initially try out different decks possible from the cards collected and find the deck which suits them the best because you need to find the most perfect deck for yourself.

You, can even create a new deck for yourself!!
